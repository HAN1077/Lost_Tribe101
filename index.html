About Us

Lost Tribe Motorcycle Gang is an established motorcycle brotherhood based in Cyberjaya, Malaysia. Founded in 2025, the organization brings together passionate riders who share a deep commitment to unity, loyalty, and the spirit of the open road. Our members ride a diverse range of motorcycles, including Harley-Davidson cruisers, custom-built machines, and high-performance sport bikes. Beyond the machines, Lost Tribe represents discipline, camaraderie, and a strong code of brotherhood that binds every member together.
